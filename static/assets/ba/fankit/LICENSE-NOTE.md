# Blue Archive Fan Kit usage note

This project should only use Blue Archive fan-kit materials within the scope allowed by the official Fan Kit precautions.

Source pages:
- https://bluearchive.jp/fankit
- https://bluearchive.jp/fankit/Precautions

Important limits summarized from the official precautions:
- Fan Kit materials are limited to wallpapers, stamps, and icons published as Fan Kit items.
- Use is limited to personal use and permitted public web/SNS-style use.
- Do not sell, rent, transfer, or use the materials commercially.
- Do not over-process or heavily modify the materials.
- Do not remove or alter copyright/trademark notices attached to the materials.

If the download script cannot reach the official site due to network/proxy restrictions, do not substitute unofficial assets into this `fankit` directory without clearly marking their source.
